**txData** is the folder where txAdmin stores its admins, configs, players, logs and etc.
<:windows:791692679419265044> **Windows location:** `txData` is in the folder that comes BEFORE/BEHIND the `FXServer.exe` file (`FXServer.exe/../txData`)
<:linux:780972840454979604> **Linux Location:** `txData` is in the folder that comes BEFORE/BEHIND the `alpine` folder (`alpine/../txData`)
